Questions

What people ask before starting

What exactly is the SHIFT Method?
SHIFT is a 6-stage behavioural financial framework — Self-Awareness, Habit Rewiring, Income Expansion, Financial Systems, Total Freedom. Unlike budgeting apps or finance courses, SHIFT addresses the psychological and behavioural root causes of financial struggle, not just the numbers. It combines a 90-day interactive workbook, live financial calculators, and 50+ AI coaching prompts.
Is the assessment really free?
Completely free. No credit card, no email required to start, no catch. You take 15 questions, discover your money pattern, and receive a personalised result showing your SHIFT starting point and an AI coaching prompt you can use immediately. At the end of your result you'll be invited to unlock the full 90-Day System — that's your choice.
How is this different from a budgeting app or finance course?
Budgeting apps assume the problem is organisation. Finance courses assume the problem is knowledge. SHIFT assumes — correctly — that the problem is behaviour. If you already know what to do but can't make yourself do it consistently, you need SHIFT, not another spreadsheet.
Does this work if I'm not based in the US or UK?
Yes — fully. The SHIFT framework is behavioural and universal. The live calculators inside the workbook support any currency. We have users across Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, India, the UAE, Canada, Australia, and Southeast Asia. Money psychology does not have a postcode.
Do I need any special tools or subscriptions to use SHIFT?
No. The workbook is a single HTML file — open it in any browser on any device, no installation, no account. The AI coaching prompts work with a free ChatGPT or Claude account. You do not need any paid subscription to get full value from SHIFT.
How much time does SHIFT take each week?
The assessment takes 3 minutes. The weekly Money Ritual at the core of the system takes 20 minutes per week. Each workbook module is designed for 30–45 minutes of focused work. Most people notice a meaningful shift in their relationship with money within the first 30 days — before they've even finished the first two stages.
